SUPERPOWER:

A superpower, in the context of fiction and imagination, refers to an extraordinary ability or skill possessed by a character that goes beyond the capabilities of normal humans. Superpowers are often featured in superhero stories, science fiction, and fantasy genres, allowing characters to perform feats that defy the laws of physics or nature. These abilities can range from enhanced strength and speed to controlling elements, reading minds, or even time travel. Superpowers are a central element in many narratives, adding excitement, conflict, and depth to characters and their interactions. They often serve as a metaphor for the human experience, exploring themes of identity, responsibility, and the choices individuals make when given extraordinary abilities.
